Target Audience Description

The target audience are a group of small business entrepreneurs looking to expand their business through our services

Scenarios

  • People will find the effective way and the requirements to be able to be part of the team and progress
  • They are planning to save for later opening a new location, but they don't know how they could facilitate those procedures without a cumbersome waiting process.
  • The fear of opening a business in the country is often due to crime and extortion, the fact of having support to be able to enjoy greater benefits facilitates trade in the country without losses in a great way.
